## Managing Tags

<div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-6 my-6">
  <div class="bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-lg p-5">
    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-gray-800 mb-3">Creating Tags</h3>
    <p class="text-gray-600 text-sm">Go to <strong>Tags</strong> in the sidebar, click <strong>Create Tag</strong>, and assign a name and color.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-lg p-5">
    <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-gray-800 mb-3">Assigning Tags</h3>
    <p class="text-gray-600 text-sm">Select tags during upload, or use <strong>Actions → Edit Tags</strong> for existing documents.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Best Practices

<div class="my-6">
  <p class="mb-3"><span class="text-green-500 mr-2">✓</span> <strong>Be Specific</strong> — Use distinct tags like <code class="bg-gray-100 px-1 rounded">invoice-us</code> vs <code class="bg-gray-100 px-1 rounded">invoice-eu</code> if they need different prompts.</p>
  <p class="mb-3"><span class="text-green-500 mr-2">✓</span> <strong>Track Status</strong> — Use tags like <code class="bg-gray-100 px-1 rounded">pending-review</code> or <code class="bg-gray-100 px-1 rounded">approved</code> for workflow stages.</p>
  <p class="mb-0"><span class="text-green-500 mr-2">✓</span> <strong>Stay Consistent</strong> — Standardize naming across your team to keep your workspace organized.</p>
</div>
