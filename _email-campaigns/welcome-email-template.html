What is Smart Document Router?

Smart Document Router is an open-source Document Understanding and Data Extraction tool that helps you process unstructured documents from faxes, email, and ERPs. It uses AI and LLMs to extract structured data while maintaining a human-in-the-loop workflow for accuracy.

Getting Started

1

Upload Your Documents

Start by uploading documents (PDFs, images, etc.) through the web interface. DocRouter supports documents from faxes, email attachments, and ERP systems.

2

Create Prompts and Schemas

Define prompts that instruct AI models on what data to extract, and create schemas to structure the output. You can use tags to automatically route documents to the right prompts.

3

Review and Verify

Use interactive forms to review and verify AI-extracted data. The human-in-the-loop approach ensures accuracy while reducing manual processing time.

4

Export and Integrate

Export your structured data or integrate with your systems using the DocRouter API. Use the Python or TypeScript SDKs for seamless integration.
